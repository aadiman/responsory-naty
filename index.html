✠ Responsory ✠

FOR NATIVIDAD (NATY) BELTRAN DIMANLIG
1 May 1929 to 14 July 2012
℣. Do not remember my sins, O Lord.
℟. When You come to judge the world by fire.
℣. Direct my way in your sight, O Lord, my God.
℟. When You come to judge the world by fire.
℣. Give Naty eternal rest, O Lord, and may your light shine on her forever.
℟. When You come to judge the world by fire.
℣. Lord, have mercy.
℟. Christ, have mercy. Lord, have mercy.
Our Father… (recited quietly or as accustomed)
℣. From the gates of hell,
℟. Deliver her soul, O Lord.
℣. May she rest in peace.
℟. Amen.
℣. Lord, hear my prayer.
℟. And let my cry come unto You.
℣. The Lord be with you.
℟. And with your spirit.
✠ let us pray ✠

Lord, welcome into your presence your daughter Naty, whom You have called from this life. Release Naty from all her sins; bless Naty with eternal light and peace, raise Naty up to live forever with all your saints in the glory of the resurrection. We ask this through Christ our Lord.

℟. Amen.

℣. Give Naty eternal rest, O Lord.
℟. And may your light shine on Naty forever.
℣. May she rest in peace.
℟. Amen.

℣. May her soul and the souls of all the faithful departed, through the mercy of God, rest in peace.

℟. Amen.
